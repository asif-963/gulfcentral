Privacy and Ploicy

Gulf Central (“we,” “our,” “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you access our website and services. By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address, and other details you provide when contacting us or using our services.
  • Business Information: Company details, trade license numbers, and documents you voluntarily submit.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, cookies, and browsing activity.
  • Transactional Information: Records of services requested, payments made, and correspondence with us.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data may be used for:

  • Providing and improving our services.
  • Responding to your inquiries and requests.
  • Processing applications, registrations, and transactions.
  • Sending service updates, newsletters, or promotional communications (only if you opt in).
  • Ensuring website security, compliance, and fraud prevention.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers & Partners: To perform services on our behalf.
  • Regulatory Authorities: Where required by law, regulation, or government request.
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tools to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You may disable cookies through your browser, but some features of the website may not function properly.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correct inaccuracies in your data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. Gulf Central is not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites.

8. Retention of Data

We will retain your information only as long as necessary for business, legal, and regulatory purposes.

9. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the United Arab Emirates.
