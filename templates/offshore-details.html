What Are The Important Documents Required For Offshore Company Setup In UAE?

  • ● Application, mainly in requisite format
  • ● Proof of valid address
  • ● Passport copy of owner as well as shareholders
  • ● Notarized MOA (Memorandum of Association)
  • ● Business plan
  • ● Professional data about the owner in curriculum vitae (CV)
  • ● Designated manager's passport copy
